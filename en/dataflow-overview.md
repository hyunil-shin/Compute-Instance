## Data & Analytics > DataFlow > Overview

* A service that allows you to easily create desired ETL (extract-transform-load) flows through drag & drop and simple configuration.
* Provides encryption filters and parsing filters that can perform simple transformation tasks on input messages.
* Provides the ability to periodically execute flows at desired times.

## Benefits

* Supports easy integration with NHN Cloud services.
* Enables safe and easy infrastructure resource management to reduce user burden.
* Plans to enhance integration with various Data Platforms and AI Services that will be added in the future.
    * Expected to be applicable in various areas such as business analytics and AI service development.

## Main Features

* You can create flows that transform data imported from original data sources through filters and deliver it to desired data sources.
* You can easily configure flow logic through drag & drop and simple configuration.
* Supports easy integration with other products within NHN Cloud.
* You can execute flows at desired times through scheduling functionality.
