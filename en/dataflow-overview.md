## Data & Analytics > DataFlow > Overview

* A service that allows you to easily create desired ETL (extract-transform-load) flows through Drag & Drop and simple configuration.
* Provides encryption filters and parsing filters that can perform simple transformation operations on input messages.
* Provides the ability to periodically execute flows at desired times.

## Benefits

* Supports easy integration with NHN Cloud services.
* Enables safe and easy infrastructure resource management that reduces the burden on users.
* We plan to strengthen integration with various Data Platforms and AI Services that will be added in the future.
    * It will be available for various uses including business analysis and AI service development.

## Main Features

* You can create flows that transform data ingested from original data sources through filters and deliver them to desired data sources.
* You can easily configure flow logic through Drag & Drop and simple settings.
* Supports easy integration with other products within NHN Cloud.
* You can execute flows at desired times through scheduling functionality.
