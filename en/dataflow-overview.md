## Data & Analytics > DataFlow > Overview

* A service that allows you to easily create desired ETL (extract-transform-load) flows with drag & drop and simple configuration.
* Provides encryption filters and parsing filters that can perform simple transformation operations on input messages.
* Provides the ability to execute flows periodically at desired times.

## Key Features

* Supports easy integration with NHN Cloud services.
* Enables safe and easy infrastructure resource management that reduces user burden.
* Plans to strengthen integration with various Data Platform and AI Services to be added in the future.
    * Will be available for use in various ways including business analysis and AI service development.

## Main Features

* You can create a flow that transforms data ingested from a source data source through filters and delivers it to the desired data source.
* Flow logic can be easily configured through drag & drop and simple setup.
* Supports easy integration with other products within NHN Cloud.
* You can execute flows at desired times through the scheduling feature.
