## Data & Analytics > DataFlow > Overview

* A service that allows you to easily create the desired ETL (extract-transform-load) flows using Drag & Drop and simple configuration.
* Provides encryption filters and parsing filters that enable you to perform simple transformation tasks on input messages.
* Provides functionality to periodically execute flows at your desired time.

## Benefits

* Supports easy integration with NHN Cloud services.
* Enables safe and convenient infrastructure resource management that reduces user burden.
* Plans to strengthen integration with various Data Platforms and AI Services to be added in the future.
    * Will be available for various uses including business analysis and AI service development.

## Key Features

* You can create flows that transform data from the original data source through filters and deliver it to the desired data source.
* You can easily configure flow logic through Drag & Drop and simple configuration.
* Supports easy integration with other NHN Cloud products.
* The scheduling feature allows you to execute flows at your desired time.
