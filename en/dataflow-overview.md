## Data & Analytics > DataFlow > Overview

* A service that enables you to easily create desired ETL (extract-transform-load) flows with drag & drop and simple configuration.
* Provides encryption filters and parsing filters that can perform simple transformation operations on input messages.
* Provides functionality to periodically execute flows at desired times.

## Benefits

* Supports easy integration with NHN Cloud services.
* Enables safe and easy infrastructure resource management with reduced user burden.
* Plans to strengthen integration with various Data Platform and AI Services to be added in the future.
    * Will be available for various applications including business analysis and AI service development.

## Key Features

* You can create flows that transform data ingested from source data sources through filters and deliver it to desired data sources.
* Flow logic can be easily configured through drag & drop and simple settings.
* Supports easy integration with other products within NHN Cloud.
* You can execute flows at desired times through scheduling functionality.
